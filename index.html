This is Miaoya’s T550 project Go Poems on making ancient Chinese poems more interactive and comprehensible for children. It contains 4 poems about Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter. This interactive scratch game is trying to provide children who are learning Chinese poems with a more vivid view of the stories to facilitate better understanding as well as an opportunity to explore the world of Scratch programming.
